## Plan K

**Budget option with cost-sharing.** Premium is low, but you pay 50% of most costs until you hit $8,000 out-of-pocket per year. Skilled nursing and hospice also 50%.

- Monthly premium range (age 65, female, non-smoker): **$73 – $183**
- Part A deductible you pay: **$868 (50%)**
- Part B deductible you pay: **$283 (you pay)**
- Part B copays/coinsurance: **10% Part B coinsurance (50% on most other items)**
- Skilled nursing facility: **Days 21-100: $108.50/day**
- Out-of-pocket maximum: **$8,000/year**
