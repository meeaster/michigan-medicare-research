## Plan L

**Mid-tier cost-sharing.** Premium is moderate, you pay 25% of most costs up to $4,000/year. Strong middle ground between K and G.

- Monthly premium range (age 65, female, non-smoker): **$138 – $341**
- Part A deductible you pay: **$434 (25%)**
- Part B deductible you pay: **$283 (you pay)**
- Part B copays/coinsurance: **5% Part B coinsurance (25% on most other items)**
- Skilled nursing facility: **Days 21-100: $54.25/day**
- Out-of-pocket maximum: **$4,000/year**
